Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

AbWork ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Health Data: With your explicit permission, AbWork accesses health data via Google Health Connect or system sensors, including step counts, heart rate, sleep data, and exercise history. This data is used solely to provide fitness insights and coaching within the app.

Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device type and OS version to ensure app stability and performance.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide personalized fitness tracking and progress visualizations.
  • To power the AI Fitness Coach for tailored nutrition and workout advice.
  • To improve app functionality and fix bugs.

3. Data Storage & Security

Most of your fitness data is stored locally on your device or securely synced via your personal Google account. We do not sell or trade your personal health data to third-party advertisers or brokers.

4. Your Permissions

You can grant or revoke access to health data at any time through your device's system settings. Revoking these permissions may limit the app's ability to provide specific fitness tracking features.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@abwork.app (Placeholder).
